Privacy Policy

RepairPartsPro collects account, subscription, and search telemetry to deliver part-search quality and service reliability.

  • We store email, hashed password, subscription status, and search activity used to improve matching quality.
  • Payment card data is handled by Stripe and never stored directly in RepairPartsPro databases.
  • We use authentication/session tokens to secure account access.
  • You may request account deletion and data removal through support.
