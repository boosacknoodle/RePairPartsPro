Who We Are

RepairPartsPro was created by repair professionals who were tired of wasting hours chasing counterfeit components, dead links, and inflated pricing. We built the tool we always wished existed: a single search that scans every major marketplace simultaneously, scores each result for authenticity, and surfaces only listings that earn a genuine certification.

We specialize in the parts most platforms quietly give up on — discontinued ICs, proprietary flex cables, donor boards, and anything a forum thread marks as "nearly impossible." If it can be found, we find it.

How It Works

1

You Search Once

Enter any part number, device model, or component description. RepairPartsPro fans that query out to every connected marketplace in parallel.

2

We Certify Every Offer

Each result is run through our Price Certification Engine, which strips aftermarket knockoffs, flags scam-signal language, and validates the seller's source type before the listing ever reaches you.

3

Rarity & Authenticity Scoring

Listings are ranked by a rarity score built from peer price variance, forum pain-intel matches, and scam-signal penalties. Hard-to-find parts bubble to the top, not paid placements.

4

Background Pain-Intel Matching

We continuously monitor repair community forums for parts that shops repeatedly struggle to source. Those signals silently boost scoring accuracy so you benefit from collective repair knowledge without any extra steps.

5

You Get a Verified Result

Only listings that pass authenticity checks appear. Every result shows a trust note, verification timestamp, and the price source so you can make a confident purchase.

Our Pledge to the Repair Community

  • We will never surface a listing we cannot stand behind — if it looks like a knockoff, it gets rejected, not ranked lower.
  • We treat "discontinued," "obsolete," and "rare" as a challenge to solve, not a reason to return zero results.
  • We absorb repair community pain signals in real time so our scoring reflects what shops are actually struggling to find today.
  • We keep pricing transparent — every result shows the source type, whether live marketplace data, dealer estimate, or reference pricing.
  • We will never charge commission or accept paid placements that change the order of genuine results.
